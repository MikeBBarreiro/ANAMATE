About

ANAMATE is a metal band, though not typical in the eyes of most metal bands. We are interested more in building a community of people by creating music for them to escape too. Our goal is to be apart of that instead of seeking the interests of what most young musicians strive for. Simply creating music that people can get lost in and love is the dream we seek. We create music ranging from expirmental progressive sounds to your generatic well structured music.ANAMATE consist of passionate musicians who are grounded in what they do for a living and expressing the passions they have through music, through ANAMATE. Express yourselv through and with us, aninamte your life with us!
